गोब्राह्मणेभ्यश्शुभमस्तु नित्यम् लोकास्समस्तास्सुखिनो भवन्तु ।।

Namo brahmanya devaya go brahmana hithaya cha! Jagadh hithaya Krishnaya govindaya namo Namah! Nivishtam gokulam yathra swasam munchathi nirbhayam! Virajayathi tham desam papam chasyapakarshathi!
With the blessings of Paramacharya, we raise and nurture Desi Cows (referred as Zebu – Bos Indicus) in South Chandler on a small farm residential community since 2020.
We started the Not for profit organization in 2024 and decided to allow this opportunity for the community to be able to perform various Sevas/Volunteering activities

Here are some of the Go Seva / Volunteering activities:

Daily/Weekly Cleaning
Daily Feeding
Making Cowdung Cakes
General Maintenance
Other Improvements
